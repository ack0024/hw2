DeLuca's Delights: Chicago's Best Italian Food Truck

While there may be many chefs in Chicago serving up Italian dishes, couple Blaise and Alexandra are putting their own spin on their relative's Southern Italian recipes all from their bright red food truck.

Try out one of the many fan favorites on Saturday Mornings at the Lincoln Park Farmer's Market!

Hours of operations: Every Saturday morning from 8am-3pm at Lincoln Park Farmer's Market
